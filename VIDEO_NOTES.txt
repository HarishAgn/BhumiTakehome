BhuMe Take-Home Video Notes

1. Problem
The cadastral plot boundaries are often shifted relative to modern satellite imagery.

2. Approach
I used the provided example truths to estimate a village-wide median shift.

3. Method
- Load plots and imagery.
- Compute centroid offsets between official plots and example truths.
- Calculate the median shift.
- Apply the shift to all plots.
- Generate predictions.geojson automatically.

4. Confidence
I used area consistency between map_area_sqm and recorded_area_sqm as a confidence signal.

5. Result
Official median IoU: 0.612
Predicted median IoU: 0.713
Improvement: +0.112

The solution is automatic and reproducible.
